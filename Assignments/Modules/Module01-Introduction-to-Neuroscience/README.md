# Module 01 — Introduction to Neuroscience

## Overview
This module introduced the fundamental concepts of neuroscience and explored how the brain serves as the biological foundation of cognition, behavior, and intelligence.

## Topics Covered

- History of neuroscience
- Structure and function of the nervous system
- Central and peripheral nervous systems
- Brain as an information-processing system
- Relationship between neuroscience and artificial intelligence

## Key Concepts Learned

In this module, I learned how the brain processes information and how neuroscience contributes to our understanding of intelligence. I explored the major components of the nervous system and the interdisciplinary connections between biology, psychology, and computational science.

## Reflection

This module established the foundation for understanding how biological intelligence inspires modern AI systems. It provided important context for the computational models explored later in the course.
